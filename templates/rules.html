Spyfall Game

Rules

🕵️ Spyfall: The No-Roles Edition!

Welcome to Spyfall, the game of mystery, bluffing, and clever questions! In this version, there are no special roles—just pure deduction and fun. Ready to play? Let’s go! 🎲

🎯 Goal of the Game:

Figure out the secret location or, if you’re the spy, try to stay hidden!

👥 What You Need:

  • 4 to 8 players 👫
  • A set of location cards (each showing the same location)
  • 1 spy card (without a location)
  • A timer (set for 8 minutes per round)

📋 Setup:

  1. Pick a set of location cards (like Beach, Hospital, Space Station).
  2. Shuffle the cards and include one spy card.
  3. Deal one card to each player. Keep it secret! 🤫

🎮 How to Play:

  • Start the Timer: Set it to 8 minutes.
  • Ask Questions:
    • Players take turns asking each other yes-or-no questions about the location.
    • Example questions:
      • “Is this place usually crowded?”
      • “Would I need special clothes here?”
  • Answer Honestly:
    • If you know the location, answer truthfully.
    • If you’re the spy, bluff your way through! 😎

🔍 Catching the Spy:

At any time, if you suspect the spy, you can call for a vote:

  1. Say, “I think [player name] is the spy!”
  2. If more than half the players agree, reveal that person’s card:
    • If they’re the spy: You win! 🎉
    • If they’re not: The spy wins! 🕵️‍♂️

🏁 End of the Round:

When time’s up, the spy reveals themselves:

  • If the spy guesses the location correctly: The spy wins! 🎉
  • If not: The other players win! 🥳

🏆 Winning the Game:

Play multiple rounds and keep score!

  • 1 point for the winning team each round.

The player or team with the most points at the end is the ultimate winner! 🏆
